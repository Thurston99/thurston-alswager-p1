The Comedy Magic of Rick Allen

Rick presents a 35-45 minute solo performing act that features interactive comedy along with mind-blowing magic.
Much of the magic is performed with borrowed objects from the audience, such as dollar bills and coats.
This, of course, leads to a lot of great situation comedy in good taste. This program would make an ideal after-dinner entertainment for that professional group.

The Strolling Magic of Rick Allen

Professional magician Rick Allen will stroll from one group to the next presenting a two to five minute presentation of knock 'em dead magic.
Rick will "wow" your guests with cards, coins, balls and other small objects. He will soon have everyone asking, "How did he do that?"
