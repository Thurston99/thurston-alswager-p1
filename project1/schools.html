Attention principals or PTA/PTO:

Here's the secret to educational assemblies that focus on topics that are relevant to your curriculum needs, yet are highly engaging!

"THE MAGIC OF BOOKS!"

This 45-minute school assembly program is ideal for K-5 audience. With personal stories and mind-blowing magic, your students will see and hear how I, as a ten-year-old boy used reading, to help achieve my goal of becoming a magician.

The show includes comical audience participation and mind blowing magic. Rick's attention-grabbing magic features live doves, a haunted antique table and for the ultimate surprise, someone from your audience will be levitated in mid-air!

During the show you can actually hear your students "ohh" and "ahh" when beautiful live doves make their magical appearance, a table seems to float, and yes, one of the kids from the audience will levitate in mid-air!

All this and more as your students will learn:

  1. The more you read, the better reader you become
  2. Read about things that interest you
  3. Start reading about things that help you achieve your dream
  4. Read about a famous person that you would like to emulate
